About Me

I am Crateiv Designer And Developer

Hi there! I'm a web designer and front-end developer from Myanmar, currently based in Japan. I came here in 2014, studied Japanese for two years, then went on to an IT college where I learned web design and programming. Since 2018, I’ve been working in the Japanese IT industry—creating websites, designing logos, and building UI screens with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and WordPress. I really enjoy small design tweaks and UI improvements—spotting those tiny details is something I’m good at. I’ve also helped train new team members and love sharing knowledge with others. Lately, I’ve been working as a Salesforce developer, handling UI layout updates and responsive design using Apex, Aura, and Visualforce. Outside of work, I enjoy doing volunteer design projects, like logos and websites for social and community groups. Design is more than just a job for me—it’s a way to connect with people and contribute to something meaningful.
